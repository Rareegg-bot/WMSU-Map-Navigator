About Us

We are a group of passionate students from Western Mindanao State University dedicated to creating useful digital solutions for our campus community. The WMSU Map Navigator was developed to help new students, transferees, visitors, and guests explore and navigate the campus more easily and confidently.


We are working on this system for 3 weeks now and this is what we came up so far, we only started learning Web Developing about 2 months ago and we are still learning as we go through this system.


Our team is committed to continuously improving the WMSU Map Navigator and welcome any input or ideas you may have.
