About Us

Western Mindanao State University is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation in Mindanao and beyond. Our campus navigation system was developed to enhance student and visitor experience by helping everyone easily find what they need and get around campus efficiently.

We constantly improve our digital tools and welcome feedback from our community to keep making navigation smooth and accessible to all.

How this works

1

Select your destination on the campus map or list.

2

Receive real-time directions and guidance.

3

Follow the step-by-step instructions to reach your destination.
